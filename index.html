About

Our life is art and art is life. This project was created for the love of the art, in all its forms, especially in painting. All these wonderful masterpieces ever created. Now it's all in front of your eyes and always with you. Artbot loves art and shares it with others. It's a very simple tool that improves your experience with art and making it closer to you. You don't even have to go to the gallery. Just push the button and see art as a film.
Stay close to the art. Stay with Artbot.
